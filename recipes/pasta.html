Pasta

Description

Indulge your taste buds in the luxurious simplicity of Creamy Garlic Parmesan Pasta. This dish combines the rich flavors of garlic and Parmesan cheese with a velvety cream sauce, creating a comforting and satisfying meal. The al dente pasta provides the perfect canvas for the luscious sauce to cling to, ensuring each bite is a delightful blend of creamy, garlicky goodness. Whether you're cooking for a cozy dinner at home or entertaining guests, this pasta recipe is sure to elevate any dining experience with its decadent yet approachable charm.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz fettuccine or your favorite pasta
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and black pepper
  • Fresh parsley, chopped

Steps

  1. Cook the Pasta: Boil a large pot of salted water and cook the pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. Prepare the Sauce: In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and saut√© for about 1-2 minutes until fragrant, being careful not to burn it.
  3. Add Cream: Pour in the heavy cream, stirring continuously. Allow the cream to simmer gently for 2-3 minutes, letting it thicken slightly.
  4. Incorporate Parmesan: Gradually add the grated Parmesan cheese to the cream sauce, stirring constantly to ensure it melts evenly. Continue to cook until the sauce is smooth and creamy.
  5. Season and Combine: Season the sauce with salt and black pepper to taste. Toss in the cooked pasta, ensuring it is well coated with the luscious garlic Parmesan sauce.
  6. Serve: Plate the creamy garlic Parmesan pasta, garnish with fresh chopped parsley, and optionally sprinkle with additional Parmesan. Enjoy this delightful dish immediately, savoring the harmonious blend of flavors in each forkful.
