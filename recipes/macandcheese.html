Mac and Cheese

Description

Indulge in the ultimate comfort food with this creamy baked macaroni and cheese recipe. A classic favorite that never fails to satisfy, this dish combines the rich flavors of gooey cheese and perfectly cooked pasta, all baked to golden perfection. The velvety cheese sauce, made from scratch, elevates the dish to a whole new level of deliciousness. Whether you're preparing a family dinner, hosting a gathering, or simply craving a comforting meal, this mac and cheese recipe is sure to become a staple in your culinary repertoire.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound (450g) elbow macaroni
  • 1/2 cup (115g) unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup (65g) all-purpose flour
  • 4 cups (960ml) whole milk
  • 2 cups (200g) sharp cheddar cheese, shredded
  • 1 cup (100g) mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 1/2 cup (50g) Parmesan cheese, grated
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste

Steps

  1. Cook the Macaroni:
    • Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
    • Cook the elbow macaroni according to the package instructions until al dente.
    • Drain and set aside.
  2. Prepare the Cheese Sauce:
    • In a large saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat.
    • Stir in the flour to create a roux, cooking for 2-3 minutes until lightly golden.
    • Gradually whisk in the milk to avoid lumps, and continue stirring until the mixture thickens.
    • Add the cheddar, mozzarella, and Parmesan cheeses, stirring until smooth and creamy.
    • Mix in the Dijon mustard, onion powder, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  3. Combine Pasta and Cheese Sauce:
    • Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
    • In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooked macaroni and the cheese sauce, ensuring the pasta is evenly coated.
  4. Bake to Perfection:
    • Transfer the mac and cheese mixture to a greased baking dish.
    • Sprinkle additional cheese on top for a golden crust.
    • Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until the top is golden and the edges are bubbly.
  5. Serve and Enjoy:
    • Allow the baked macaroni and cheese to cool for a few minutes before serving.
    • Garnish with a sprinkle of fresh parsley if desired.
    • Dive into the creamy, cheesy goodness and savor the irresistible comfort of homemade mac and cheese.
